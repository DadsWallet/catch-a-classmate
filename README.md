# Nikos: Fall of Troy 3D

Simple standalone 3D story game (separate from your existing RPG project), based on your Nikos/Troy/Trojan-horse story.

## Run

1. Open `/Users/adonis/Library/CloudStorage/OneDrive-ThePittwaterHouseSchool/Documents/StoryRunner3D/index.html` in a browser.
2. Press `Start Adventure`.

## This version

- 50% harder: more frequent obstacles, denser lane blocks, and 50% higher hit damage
- 50% longer: chapter goals scaled up and expanded to 5 story chapters

## Controls

- Move lanes: `A` / `D` or arrow keys
- Jump: `Space`
- Restart anytime: `R`
- Mobile: use on-screen `Left`, `Jump`, and `Right` buttons
